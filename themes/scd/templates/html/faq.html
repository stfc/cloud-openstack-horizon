1. How do I login?

You can SSH into your machines with your federal credentials eg. ssh fed12345@vm20.nubes.stfc.ac.uk. You can also associate your SSH public key to your account for passwordless login by creating or importing a keypair here


2. Can I get root access?

Yes, once you have logged in with your federal credentials you will have permission to use sudo.

NOTE: you must not change the root password or remove entires from the authorized_keys file for the root account. Please make sure you have read the Terms of Service.


3. Can I get a GUI session?

Click the dropdown under Actions and select Console to get an interactive GUI session inside your browser.


4. Can I increase the RAM or CPUs of a Virtual Machine?

We provide a standard set of flavors for Virtual Machines. If you would like to other combinations of resources, please email us at cloud-support@helpdesk.gridpp.rl.ac.uk. An existing virtual machine can have its resources increased by powering off the VM and selecting resize in the Actions menu.


5. Can I increase my group's quota?

Yes, if you would like your quota increased email us at cloud-support@helpdesk.gridpp.rl.ac.uk.


6. Can I get more disk space?

Yes, larger flavors come with more disk space. You can also create a volume and attach this to your instance. cloud-support@helpdesk.gridpp.rl.ac.uk.


7. Do you support other Linux variants/version?

Yes, we support uploading your own images to the SCD Cloud however in doing this you become responsible for maintaining these images and ensuring that they comply with the SCD Cloud Terms of Service. Please contact us to ask if we already provide a suitable image at cloud-support@helpdesk.gridpp.rl.ac.uk.


8. Why are Pakiti and rsyslog running on my VM?

These services are used for monitoring and security. Please refrain from stopping or changing the behaviour of either of these services. Please make sure you have read the Terms of Service .
